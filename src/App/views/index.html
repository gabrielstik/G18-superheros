WHAT IS JARVIS ?
Do you dream of seeing your favorite heroes compete in unusual fights? Go on an adventure with this card game and develop strategies to win and appear in the leaderboard!
Superheroes are waiting for you!

HOW TO PLAY ?
JARVIS is a challenge and strategy game. The goal of the game is to reduce the life of your opponent's hero to zero to win the game.
It's a multi-player card game, you can play against a random opponent or you can challenge a friend!
